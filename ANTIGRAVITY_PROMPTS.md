## Usage Notes

- **Negative prompts to always add:** `watermark, text, logo, signature, blurry, 
  low quality, jpeg artefacts, overexposed, lens flare, cartoon, illustration style`
- **Style modifiers that work well:** `octane render · cinema4d · unreal engine 5 · 
  photorealistic · 8K · ACES filmic · dark studio lighting · no text`
- **Seed all hero images with the same seed** for visual consistency across pages
- **Export format:** PNG, minimum 2000px wide for web use
- All prompts are designed to produce **dark-background images** that integrate 
  directly into the `#080808` site background without masking or compositing
